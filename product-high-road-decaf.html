High Road Decaf proves decaf doesn't have to be an afterthought. Sourced from Colombia's Huila region and decaffeinated using the chemical-free Swiss Water Process, it retains the full flavor character of the original bean. Cocoa, dried cherry, and a smooth, mellow finish. Your afternoon menu just got an upgrade.

High Road Decaf is forgiving across brew methods. Treat it like any medium roast — drip, pour-over, or espresso all work well. Water at 93°C, medium grind, 1:15 ratio. Because caffeine affects extraction slightly, you may need to grind just a touch finer than your regular medium.
