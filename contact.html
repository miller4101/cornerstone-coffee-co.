There is no minimum for our Starter tier. Case and bulk pricing starts at 5 bags per order. We built it this way so small cafes can get started without committing to more than they need.
Every order is roasted within 48 hours of shipping. We don't pre-roast and stock — your beans go from roaster to bag to your door as quickly as possible.
