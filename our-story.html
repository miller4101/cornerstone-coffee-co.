— How It Started —

Built for the Small Guys.

Cornerstone Coffee Co. was founded with one simple idea: small cafe owners should have access to the same quality beans that the big chains enjoy, at pricing that actually makes sense for a small business.

Too many independent cafes were stuck choosing between quality they couldn't afford and affordability that compromised their menu. We knew there had to be a better way.

So we built a direct wholesale model — partnering with responsible growers, roasting in small batches for consistency, and keeping our overhead lean so we could pass the savings on to the cafes we serve.

Our Values

Every bean we use is sourced from growers we trust — people who take pride in their craft and treat their workers fairly.

Roasted for Consistency

Small-batch roasting lets us control every variable. Your customers get the same great cup on their first visit and their hundredth.
